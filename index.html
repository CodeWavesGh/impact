About Us



Smile Impact Ghana Foundation is a non-profit organization
committed to Community Development,Education and Mental Health Advocacy.


It was established on the 18th February,2021 by Miss Princess Jasmine Mckenzie (Ghana) and Gregory Conna Dododzah (USA).

Learn More About Our Core Values, Mission And Vision
At Smile Impact Ghana Foundation

Our Core Values

Integrity, Continuos Improvement,
Innovation & Team Work.

Our Mission

To work relentlessly toward Development and bring social
changes in Ghana by playing a critical role in improving citizen
participation through mental health advocacy initiatives.

Our Vision

To create cohesive and vibrant community by representing
with Integrity, fairness, humanity, listening to voices unheard and
recognizing potentials where others see despair.

How We Work

Smile Impact Ghana Foundation has undertaken three major projects annually

Mental Health Advocacy

Our Team embarks on Mental
Health Campus tours and
Community Education yearly.

Community Service Outreach

Annually we deploy volunteers to rural communities
to render immediate support
in these key areas: Medicine, Education and Eco Tourism.

Christmas Inclusion

Every Christmas season, our Team visits schools and
institutions for people
with disabilities and special needs. We donate basic necessities like
groceries, cloths, educational materials and toiletries.
